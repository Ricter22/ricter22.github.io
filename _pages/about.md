## Hi, I'm Riccardo

I'm from Pescara, Italy—a coastal town where I spent my childhood reading Greek tragedies and shooting basketballs. Still doing both, though the shooting's improved since then.

**The Academic Odyssey (aka "Why I Can't Pick One Country")**

I studied Computer and Automation Engineering at Marche Polytechnic University in Ancona, but couldn't resist the Erasmus temptation, so I visited Reutlingen Hochschule in Germany first. Then I decided one Italian university wasn't enough and moved to Politecnico di Milano for a Master's in Computer Science and Engineering. For my thesis, I took another Erasmus adventure to the University of Southern Denmark—apparently I collect European universities like Pokémon.

Now I'm a PhD student at SDU (still in Denmark, apparently I got attached), where I specialize in AI, LLMs, RAG, Agents, and Information Retrieval. Basically, I make computers talk to themselves in increasingly sophisticated ways.

**When I'm Not Thinking About AI**

I still mess around with basketball whenever I find time. My shot selection has improved slightly since my Pescara days, though my ankles would argue with you. Still reading as many books as I can, mixing classic and contemporary literature at the moment.
